Projects

One project I worked on was a bigram language model. It learns English word pairings from "Alice in Wonderland", and generates text by selecting one of the next most likely word continuously.

It's an improvement from a previous design that used pre-processed bigram word pairings from the internet.

That design had no filter to it and could generate some interesting outputs, to say the least.
